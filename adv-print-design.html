The Godavari Pulasa is not just a fish — it is a seasonal event in Andhra Pradesh. Available only during the monsoon floods, it is prized for its taste and carries deep cultural weight in the delta communities.

From its scale patterns to its silhouette against the river, the Pulasa offered a rich visual vocabulary to work with — one that felt both local and timeless.

Motif Development

Sketch to stamp

Each motif was refined from the drawings — simplified into printable units that could tile, repeat, and breathe together on the cloth. Scales, silhouettes, fins, and water ripples each became their own motif family.
