The Brief

What takes
you back?

"List the things that make you feel nostalgic — that carry you back to childhood."

From that list, I chose the Aglaonema Lipstick — a houseplant I grew up seeing. Its deep crimson leaves veined with olive green felt familiar, tactile, alive. The brief became a translation of that sensory memory into fabric.
