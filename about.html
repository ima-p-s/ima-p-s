Hello,

I’m Ima. I am a Textile and Apparel Design student at the National Institute of Design, Andhra Pradesh, currently in my 6th semester.

My love for hands-on work and my patience are what brought me into textile design. I truly enjoy the slow, steady process of building something from scratch—starting with just a thread and turning it into a finished piece of fabric.

In textile I'm more into weaving, dyeing, and fabric construction. I enjoy experimenting with colours and textures to see how they affect the feel of a material. For me, design is a blend of materials, colours, and emotions. I find inspiration in the world around me and care deeply about working in a sustainable way. Each project gives me a new opportunity to learn, explore, and share a story through my craft.

This portfolio is a collection of those stories and the journey I am taking as a designer.
