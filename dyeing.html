Cycle of
Emotions

A textile art exploring the emotional journey of love — expressed through shibori dyeing on fabric.

Nui Shibori Itajme Shibori Arashi Shibori Natural Dye Chemical Dye
The Concept

Love is not one feeling.
It is all of them.

Love
Warm, consuming, full of possibility.
Happiness
Golden, radiant, alive.
Vulnerability
Fear of losing what matters.
Hatred
Dark, heavy, suffocating.
Sadness
Blue, quiet, deep.
Love again
The courage to begin once more.

A person goes through different emotions in the process of loving someone — from warmth to happiness, then the fear of losing it, vulnerability, things turning ugly, and finally the courage to move on and love again.

Each fabric panel represents one stage of this cycle. The colours, patterns, and textures were chosen deliberately — translated through shibori technique into feeling. Life goes on.

Techniques

The language
of resistance & dye

01
Nui Shibori
Stitching and gathering fabric before dyeing. Creates soft, organic resist patterns.
02
Itajme Shibori
Folding and clamping fabric between boards. Produces sharp geometric patterns.
03
Arashi Shibori
Wrapping fabric diagonally around a pole. Creates flowing diagonal stripe patterns.
Dye Type 01
Natural Dyeing
Plant-based dyes, organic colour
Dye Type 02
Chemical Dyeing
Reactive dyes, bold saturated colour
