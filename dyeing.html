A person goes through different emotions in the process of loving someone — from warmth and happiness, to fear, vulnerability, and darkness. Then the courage to move on, and love again. Life goes on.

Each fabric panel represents one stage. The colour, pattern, and texture of each piece was chosen deliberately — translated through shibori into feeling.
