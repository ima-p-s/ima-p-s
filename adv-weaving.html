Fabric Construction → Advanced Weaving

In Advanced Weaving, selected samples from the Fabric Construction project became the foundation for a new work. The colours and textures of the Aglaonema Lipstick palette — carried forward through end and end warp — grew into a full textile art.

While the course explored double cloth and extra warp techniques, the piece incorporates warp rib — a technique I noticed in the structure of my own bag, recognising the same end and end logic in an everyday object.
